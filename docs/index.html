internetipstooltips without the whole kitchen sink

Why another tooltip library? We needed something lightweight with minimal assumptions about integration, as well as dynamic repositioning (viewport edge detection), and pointer following. Most other libraries including these features were quite heavy, so we wrote our own.

Why is it called internetips? 'Cause every other tooltip thing has "tool" in it, and that gets old.
